Sisal Immersive Museum
The Sisal Immersive Museum is an interactive space designed to showcase the story and heritage of Sisal. The experience allows users to explore how the company has evolved over time, recreating past environments and shops. I worked on the creation of various assets used in both the library environment and the betting shop, where people once placed their bets..

3D Modeling, Texturing
The betting shop was the most carefully designed environment. The goal was to make the user feel as if they were inside an old betting shop, complete with blackboards and tables displaying match results written by hand. The texturing process was fundamental in achieving this, aiming to convey a vintage feel without making it look artificially aged.
